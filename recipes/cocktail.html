Pomegranate Juice
Pomegranate juice is a beverage made from
the fruit of the pomegranate (Punica granatum).
Pomegranates are large, round fruits with a tough
outer skin and juicy seeds known as arils.
The juice is extracted by pressing or squeezing
the arils, yielding a deep red, sweet, and tart liquid.


A pomegranate cocktail is a mixed drink that incorporates
pomegranate juice or pomegranate-flavored ingredients
as a key component. Pomegranate cocktails are known for their
vibrant color, sweet and tart flavor, and refreshing taste.
They are often enjoyed as a fruity and festive beverage for
special occasions or as a refreshing cocktail choice year-round.


Ingredients


  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz pomegranate juice
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup.

Instructions to be followed:


  1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Garnish with a twist of lime or a few pomegranate arils.
